 Slack Info

The following channels are available to join on the flatiron-school.slack.com Slack workspace. (This is a different workspace than Flatiron-Community, which was used during Pre-Work.)

Phase-Specific Channels to Join

  • #ds_phase_1 - Channel to discuss coding in Python + SQL, data manipulation + analysis, and data gathering
  • #ds_phase_2 - Channel to discuss statistical modeling and linear regression.
  • #ds_phase_3 - Channel to discuss machine learning, classification algorithms, and building modeling pipelines.
  • #ds_phase_4 - Channel to discuss big data, recommendation systems, time series modeling, natural language processing, neural networks, and AWS.
  • #ds_phase_5 - Channel to discuss the data science capstone project.

Note tag/mention @ds-support-squad in your message on the above Slack channels if you have a specific question that you'd like an instructor to answer ASAP. 

Flatiron-Wide Community Channels

  • #student-blogs -  a space where you can post your blogs and get feedback in order to improve your writing.
  • #current-events-in-tech - weekly discussions on current tech news to increase your knowledge and strengthen your industry-based communication skills.
